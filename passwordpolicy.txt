Aim: Prepare Password Policy for Computer and Mobile Device
Theory:
A password policy is a set of rules and guidelines that define the requirements and
restrictions for creating and managing passwords within an organization or system. Password
policies aim to enhance security by ensuring that users create strong and unique passwords,
regularly update them, and adhere to secure password practices.
Key components of a password policy may include:
1. Password length requirements
2. Complexity requirements (e.g., use of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, special
characters)
3. Password expiration and renewal frequency
4. Prohibition of password reuse
5. Account lockout thresholds
6. Multi-factor authentication requirements
